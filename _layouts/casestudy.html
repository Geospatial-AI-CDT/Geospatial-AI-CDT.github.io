Our approach to designing a training programme for the CDT is based around student stories. We focus on how we envisage that our CDT has allowed the students to reach their goals. These case studies are not entirely hypothetical; they are based around knowledge of previous students, and serve as a useful way to identify where existing gaps are and how a cohort-based training approach will address these.
